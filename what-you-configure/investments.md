# Investments

For companies that invest in other companies. Track how many investments they've made, where they invest, which industries and funding stages they focus on. All configurable fields that match against your criteria.

{% hint style="info" %}
Investment activity reveals strategic priorities. Partners use it to identify active acquirers, corporate venture arms, or companies signaling market expansion through their portfolio.
{% endhint %}

## Fields

| Field | Description | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Number of investments made that match \* | Number of investments made by the company matching your criteria | 8 |
| List of countries invested in that match \* | Countries where matching investments are located | United States, Israel, United Kingdom |
| List of Crunchbase industries invested in that match \* | Industries of investments made by the company matching your criteria | SaaS, FinTech, HealthTech |
| List of funding stages invested in that match \* | Investment stages of investments made by the company matching your criteria | Seed, Series A |
| List of LinkedIn industries invested in that match \* | Industries of companies the company has invested in, matching your criteria | Computer Software, Financial Services |
