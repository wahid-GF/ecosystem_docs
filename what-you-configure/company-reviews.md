# Company Reviews

Customer-facing reviews and ratings. These are configurable fields that match against your keyword criteria, so you'll only see results for the terms you've set up.

## Fields

| Field | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Has reviews that match \* | Whether a review with a keyword match exists |
| Number of reviews that match \* | Count of reviews matching your configured keyword criteria |

> Fields marked with \* are configurable and depend on your dataset setup.
