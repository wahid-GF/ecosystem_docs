# Hiring

Everything about a company's current hiring activity. Total open roles, where they're hiring, keyword-matched job postings, and how much of their hiring happens outside their HQ or office locations. One of the most time-sensitive signal groups in the dataset.

## Keyword matches

| Field | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Has jobs that match | Whether open roles with keyword matches exist |
| Number of jobs that match | Count of open roles matching your configured keyword criteria |
| Percentage of jobs that match | Percentage of open roles with keyword matches relative to all open roles |
| List of countries with jobs that match | Countries where open roles with keyword matches are located |
| List of keywords in jobs descriptions that match | Keywords found in open role descriptions matching your configuration |

## Open roles

| Field | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Total number of open jobs | Total number of unique open roles currently listed |
| Open jobs in last 3 months | Count of total open roles listed in the last 3 months |
| Open jobs countries | List of countries where open roles are located |
| Open jobs countries count | Count of countries where open roles are located |

## Geographic distribution

| Field | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Hiring countries outside office locations | Countries outside company office locations where jobs are published |
| Hiring countries outside office locations count | Number of countries outside company office locations where jobs are published |
| Jobs outside office locations count | Number of jobs in countries outside company office locations |
| Jobs outside office locations percentage | Percentage of jobs published in countries outside company office locations relative to all published jobs |
| Jobs outside HQ count | Number of jobs in countries outside company HQ |
| Jobs outside HQ percentage | Percentage of jobs published outside company HQ relative to all published jobs |

> **Shared fields**: Open jobs countries and Hiring countries outside office locations also appear in [Location](location.md).
